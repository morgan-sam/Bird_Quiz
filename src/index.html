How To Play:

The quiz retrieves images of birds from Wikipedia which must be matched to the corresponding common bird name. You win or lose points depending on whether you give correct or incorrect answers. Each game has 10 randomly selected questions.

There are three different quiz modes:

  • In the first quiz a single image is shown that must be matched to one of 4 bird names. You have unlimited time to answer and can attempt to answer as many times as you like. The more incorrect attempts you make the more points you lose.
  • In the second quiz a single name is shown that must be matched to one of 4 bird images. You have unlimited time to answer and can attempt to answer as many times as you like. The more incorrect attempts you make the more points you lose.
  • In the third quiz each question will randomly chose between both styles of the former 2 quizzes, except this time you will have a 10 second time limit and only one shot at choosing the right answer per question.

At the end of every quiz you score will be converted into a percentage score. Work your way up through each quiz and try to beat the highscore for each.

If the quiz accidentally includes any incorrect images such as pictures of maps instead of images of birds, please use the report image button to remove the incorrect image from the database. On reporting the image the quiz will reset the question and your score will remain unaffected.
